Tips

Important Tips to Maintain RO

It's no secret that clean drinking water is essential to keep oneself healthy. Here are a few tips on how to maintain your water purifier

Self Care
Cleaning and Sanitization

Read & follow the User Manual. Keep the exteriors of the RO System clean. Check for leakages

Maintenance
Service Regularly

The most important part of a water purifier is the water filter. Get it cleaned at regular interval

Replacing Parts
Replace Filters & Membrane

Regular servicing by a professional technician keeps the purifier running at optimum. Replace the filters & RO Membrane at regular interval or as suggested by the OEM
